Welcome to the Nearby Restaurant Recommendations Blog!

Are you looking for place near campus to grab a bite? Then look no further than this blog post! We will give you recommendations on foods and drinks that you should try out if you're on campus. From hot dogs to milkshakes, pizzas, and hot subs, we got you covered. We will add more recommendations later as more students try out different things, but for now, we will give 3 foods to try out.

Food Recommendations

Yum Yum Better Ice Cream

Website: Yum Yum Better Ice Cream

Location: 1219 Spring Garden St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Specialties: Ice cream, hot dogs, and milkshakes

Hours:
Monday: 10AM-9PM
Tuesday-Friday: 10:30AM-9PM
Saturday: 10:30AM-5PM
Sunday: Closed

If you want to delicious and cheap fast food, go to Yum Yums!

Slices Pizza by Tony

Website: Slices Pizza by Tony

Location: 401 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Specialties: Pizzas, hot and cold subs, pastas, and Italian dessets

Hours:
Monday-Friday: 10:30AM-10PM
Saturday: 11AM-10PM
Sunday: Closed

If you want to try pizzas and other Italian dishes, check out Slices Pizzaby Tony! I tried them out for the first time during my sophomore year and have always gone back.

Chick-fil-A

Website: Chick-fil-A

Location: 516 Stirling St, Greensboro, NC 27412 (Floor 1 of the Elliot University Center)

Specialties: Chicken sandwiches and waffle potato fries.

Hours:
Monday-Thrusday: 7:30AM-10PM
Friday: 7:30AM-5PM
Saturday: 10:30AM-6PM
Sunday: Closed

For classic southern style chicken between two buns, come to Chick-fil-A! They are famours for their chicken sandwiches.
