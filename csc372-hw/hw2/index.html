Welcome to Granny's Pie! We are a small and family owned business
based in Burlington, North Carolina. Here you will find a lemon pie recipe
so delicious that it has won the in the top 3 spot of the
National Pie Championship 4 years in a row! It is our classic lemon meringue pie recipe!
This recipe was created by Granny, the founder of the business.

Drinks that go well with the lemon meringue pie

  • Coffee: Nothing beats a classic cup of joe.
  • Milk: Plain milk always goes well with a sweet dessert
  • Chocolate milk: If you want to try something else other than plain milk, you can't go wrong with this one.
  • Cappucino: If you want the best of both coffee and milk, then cappucino is the drink for you.
  • Lemonade: It only makes sense to drink a lemon based refreshment along with a lemon based dessert! Try it if you want a nice change from coffee or milk based drinks.
